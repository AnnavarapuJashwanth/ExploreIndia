Must Visit Places in India

Arunachalam

Arunachalesvara Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Shiva.

Pondicherry

Pondicherry is the capital of the Union Territory of Puducherry in India.

Goa

Known for its beaches, culture, and historical places.

Araku Valley

A picturesque hill station in Andhra Pradesh.

Munnar

A beautiful town in Kerala's Western Ghats mountain range.

Manali

A resort town in Himachal Pradesh, best visited from October to February.

Rameswaram

A pilgrimage destination home to the Ramanathaswamy Temple.

Taj Mahal

An ivory-white marble mausoleum on the Yamuna riverbank in Agra.
